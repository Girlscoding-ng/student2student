Student2Student

It is a platform that brings students together to learn,improve,educate and enhance the spirit of team work.

Why This Website ?

This website is a platform for young students who are having problems with their studies and interaction with their teachers.

It will help to improve better in their studies,help each other with their difficulties and make them ready for their future.

Our Target

Our focus is mainly on Senior Secondary School (SSS) students because they are taking their very first bold step into their future and decide what to make of their life with what they learn now.

SSS1

This section introduces the beginners to the core subjects that are to be learnt in sss acording to their department.

SSS2

This section takes the middle class on subjects they have been introduced to in their first year and prepares them for their final year.

SSS3

This section prepares the student for their final year examination and it helps for the course they will study in the higher institution.
